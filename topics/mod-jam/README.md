# Frog Feaster 3000

## Overview

Frog Frenzy is an interactive p5.js game where you control a frog to catch flies and avoid a raging horsefly and distractions.  
Click to shoot the frog’s tongue, eat flies to score points, and survive as long as possible.

## How to Play

- Move your mouse to control the frog’s position.
- Click to launch the tongue and catch flies.
- Avoid the horsefly, getting hit adds a strike.
- Every 5 flies: frog croaks, confetti appears, and the horsefly becomes faster.
- The game ends after 3 strikes.

## Features

- Animated frog, tongue, and background video
- Sound effects for eating, croaking, and damage
- Confetti milestone animation
- UI showing flies eaten and strikes
- Title and game-over screens
